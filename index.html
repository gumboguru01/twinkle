About Us

Twinkle Mobile – Your Trusted Tech & Gadget Store

Twinkle Mobile was established in 2020 and officially launched its first shop in November 2021. From humble beginnings with a small table at PG Complex, we have grown into a trusted name in tech retail, now operating two branches in Kwekwe:

  • Shop 19, Tauro Tragus Centre (Upstairs), Dollar Motors Service Station
  • Shop A2, Miracle Mall (Ground Floor)

We offer a wide range of original and exclusive products at unbeatable prices, serving both walk-in and online customers. Our success is rooted in partnerships with schools, organizations, and the community—fueled by strategic marketing, door-to-door outreach, and a deep commitment to customer satisfaction.
